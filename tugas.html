What is coffee?

"Coffee" is a drink made from coffee beans that have been roasted and processed. Coffee beans come from coffee trees that grow in different parts of the world, especially in the tropics. The process of making coffee involves roasting the coffee beans, grinding, and brewing with hot water. Coffee has a variety of varieties and brewing methods that produce a variety of unique flavors, aromas, and characteristics.

How to order a menu at AromaCoffee

When ordering coffee at a café, the first step that needs to be done is to choose the type of coffee you want to enjoy. After that, also choose other options such as size (for example, small, medium, or large) and whether you want the coffee to be served hot or cold. If you have a particular preference, such as wanting unsweetened coffee or with almond milk, be sure to bring it up to the barista.
